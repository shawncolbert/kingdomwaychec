What We Believe

Our Foundation of Faith

One Eternal God

We believe there is One Eternal, Almighty, and True God — Creator and Sustainer of heaven and earth — manifesting Himself in three distinct Persons.

Father, Son & Holy Spirit

Each Person of the Godhead possesses precisely the same nature, attributes, and perfections, and is worthy of the same reverence, worship, and love.

Fully God, Fully Man

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully Man — both in His undiminished Deity and unfallen humanity, united without confusion within His glorious Person.

The Atonement

In infinite love for the lost, Jesus Christ became the divinely provided, sacrificial Lamb. His death was substitutionary — the Just for the unjust — abolishing the curse of the Law.

Bodily Resurrection

We believe in the bodily resurrection of the Lord Jesus Christ — the archetype of the believer's future resurrected body, full of flesh, bones, glory, and immortality.

Living Hope

The resurrection is a living reality that anchors our faith, transforms our present lives, and gives confident hope for eternity. Because He lives, we shall live also.

Grace Alone

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, and in Christ alone — the Triune God's free sovereign gift that cannot be earned by human works.

A New Nature

This new life is implanted by the Holy Spirit through the Word. No manner of good works can ever improve upon this gracious bestowment of man's new nature in Christ Jesus.
